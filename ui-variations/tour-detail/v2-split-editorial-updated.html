About This Safari

The Ultimate Tanzania Experience

This carefully crafted 7-day journey takes you through Tanzania's most spectacular wildlife destinations. From the endless plains of the Serengeti to the breathtaking Ngorongoro Crater, every day brings new adventures and unforgettable encounters.

Travel in comfort in our custom 4x4 Land Cruisers with pop-up roofs for 360-degree game viewing. Your expert safari guide knows exactly where to find the action - from lion prides on the hunt to the Great Migration river crossings.

"Watching a leopard descend from an acacia tree at sunset, with the Serengeti stretching endlessly behind - that's a moment that stays with you forever."

Stay in hand-picked lodges and luxury tented camps that blend seamlessly with the wilderness. Wake up to the sounds of the bush, enjoy sundowners overlooking the savanna, and fall asleep to the distant calls of hyenas.
