About This Route

The Whiskey Route Experience

The Machame Route is Kilimanjaro's most popular climbing path, and for good reason. Its varied terrain and excellent acclimatization profile give climbers the best chance of reaching the summit while experiencing the mountain's full beauty.

Over seven days, you'll journey through five distinct ecological zones: from the lush rainforest alive with colobus monkeys, through the heath and moorland, across the alpine desert, and finally to the arctic summit zone.

"Standing on Uhuru Peak at sunrise, with the glaciers glowing gold around you, you understand why they call this the Roof of Africa."

The route follows the "climb high, sleep low" principle that's proven most effective for altitude acclimatization. You'll trek to Lava Tower at 4,600m before descending to sleep at Barranco Camp, giving your body the best preparation for summit night.
