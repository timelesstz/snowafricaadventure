Overview

The Machame Route, affectionately known as the "Whiskey Route", is Kilimanjaro's most popular climbing path. This 7-day itinerary offers excellent acclimatization through the proven "climb high, sleep low" approach, giving you the best chance of reaching Uhuru Peak.

You'll traverse diverse ecosystems from lush rainforest to alpine desert, experience the famous Barranco Wall scramble, and witness breathtaking views of both Kibo and Mawenzi peaks. Our professional guides have led thousands of successful summits on this route.
