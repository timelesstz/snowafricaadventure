The Scenic Whiskey Route

The Machame Route is the most popular and scenic path to Kilimanjaro's summit. Known as the "Whiskey Route" for its steep, challenging terrain, it rewards climbers with breathtaking views and diverse landscapes.

Your 7-day journey traverses five distinct ecological zones - from lush montane rainforest through alpine desert to the glaciated summit. The "climb high, sleep low" profile ensures optimal acclimatization and our industry-leading 92% success rate.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your climb

Good cardiovascular fitness is essential. Start training 3-4 months before with hiking, stair climbing, and cardio. If you can hike 10km comfortably with elevation gain, you're ready.
Our 7-day itinerary maximizes acclimatization. Guides monitor climbers twice daily, and we carry emergency oxygen. Consider Diamox as prevention.
Best seasons are January-March and June-October for stable weather. January-March is warmer; June-October is drier.
Join scheduled group departures or book private climbs. Solo travelers welcome in group departures - great for meeting fellow adventurers.
