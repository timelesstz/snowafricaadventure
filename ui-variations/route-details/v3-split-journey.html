Route Overview

The Machame Route, known as the "Whiskey Route" for its steep and challenging terrain, is considered one of the most beautiful and successful paths to Uhuru Peak. Starting from the south, this 7-day journey takes you through lush rainforest, alpine moorland, and arctic summit zones.

Your Journey

Day 1

Machame Gate to Machame Camp

+1,190m

Begin at Machame Gate (1,800m) and trek through pristine montane rainforest. Watch for blue monkeys and exotic birds as you ascend through moss-covered trees to Machame Camp.

11km 5-7 hours Camp: 2,990m
Day 2

Machame Camp to Shira Cave

+890m

Exit the rainforest into stunning heathland with views of Shira Plateau. The terrain becomes more rugged as you traverse moorland dotted with giant lobelias.

5km 4-6 hours Camp: 3,880m
Day 3

Shira to Lava Tower to Barranco

+680m -680m

Critical acclimatization day! Ascend to Lava Tower (4,630m) for lunch, then descend to Barranco Camp. This "climb high, sleep low" approach dramatically improves summit success.

10km 6-8 hours Camp: 3,950m
Day 6

Summit Day - Uhuru Peak!

5,895m

Begin at midnight under the stars. Push through Stella Point to witness a spectacular sunrise from Africa's highest point. An unforgettable achievement awaits!

16km 12-15 hours Uhuru Peak!

Common Questions

While no technical climbing is required, good cardiovascular fitness is essential. We recommend starting a training program 3-4 months before your climb, focusing on hiking, stair climbing, and cardio exercises. If you can comfortably hike 10km with elevation gain, you're on the right track.
Our 7-day itinerary is specifically designed to maximize acclimatization. Guides monitor all climbers twice daily, and we carry emergency oxygen. The "climb high, sleep low" profile gives your body the best chance to adapt. Consider asking your doctor about Diamox as a preventive measure.
The best seasons are January-March and June-October when weather is most stable. January-March is warmer, while June-October is drier. We run climbs year-round, but these periods offer the clearest summit views and most comfortable trekking conditions.
You can join one of our scheduled group departures or book a private climb for you and your companions. Solo travelers are welcome to join our group departures, which is a great way to meet fellow adventurers. Private climbs can depart on any date with minimum 2 people.
